About PlantHAT

I started this personal project to monitor the growth of my tomatoes and other vegetables and I also wanted to automate the watering based on simple strategies.

Just mount the board on top of a Raspberry Pi (or connect your own boards to the GPIOs), connect it to the local Internet/WLAN and use your tablet, phone or PC to check in on your plants. It automatically controls multiple pumps and valves based on strategies that you can set to either optimize your vegetable yield or to take care of precious plants while you are gone.

Click the "Login" button, create a local account through the "Register" link and start controlling your plants growth!
